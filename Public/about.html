Our Mission

Fulfilling the gap in the market

After taking the time to evaluate the existing cryptocurrency information sites in the market, we have identified an opportunity to serve the unfulfilled need for hot wallet recommendation in the market, serving crypto investors that are looking beyond the CEX (Centralized Exchange) but are having the difficulty to install the hot wallet and subsequent blockchain network that fits their investment needs.

Our subsequent inclusion of a TechEd corner and Crypto index also serves our end goal of being a public web service that helps lower the hurdle for crypto investors to explore decentralized exchanges (DEX) by enhancing their accessibility to wallet and network recommendations, as well as to comprehensive information of different cryptocurrencies and educational materials.
